Roll Dice app:

Display a die somewhere on your screen. When the user taps on the die, it should randomly
switch to another number.
Keep count of the number of times each number is rolled. Somewhere on the screen, display
how many times each face has been rolled.
Include a button that resets the counts for how many times each face has been rolled.

Extra Credit
  • Include a Switch that changes the color of your die between red and white.
  • Sound effects.
  • Animations
